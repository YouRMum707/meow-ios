Build 2026061402 (1.3.2)

WHAT'S NEW
• Fixed QUIC / HTTP-3 apps (notably Xiaohongshu / RedNote) failing to
  load or bypassing the proxy. Their HTTPS DNS records were handing the
  app the origin's real IP, so HTTP-3 connections went straight out
  instead of through the tunnel — breaking routing for exactly those
  apps, and stalling on slow lookups. Those address hints are now
  stripped in fake-IP mode, so the connection stays on the proxy.
• The tunnel is now IPv4-only. The TUN no longer claims IPv6 and AAAA is
  suppressed, so every connection rides the proxied IPv4 path.

FOR TESTERS
• Xiaohongshu / RedNote: open the app, scroll the feed, load images and
  videos — content should load normally over the proxy.
• General browsing and streaming should be unaffected; please report any
  site or app that worked before but now stalls or fails to load.
